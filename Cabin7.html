Chapter 1: The Midnight Train

Mark loves his job as a journalist, but it often requires him to work late into the night. Tonight is no exception. It is 11:30 PM when he finally leaves his office in the city center. Outside, the weather is terrible. A heavy rain is falling, and a cold wind blows through the empty streets. Mark pulls his jacket tight and walks quickly toward the train station.

The station is unusually quiet. The grand platform, usually crowded with hundreds of rushing people, is completely deserted. Mark looks up at the digital timetable screen. Most of the lights are off, but one line flashes in amber: *11:45 PM – Final Express to Oakwood*. This is his train. He buys a ticket from a machine and walks down the dark platform to board the very last carriage.

Strona 2

When Mark steps inside the carriage, a strange feeling of unease creeps over him. The interior is brightly lit by flickering fluorescent lights, but it is freezing cold. More importantly, it is completely empty. There are no teenagers listening to music, no business people reading newspapers, and not even a conductor. Mark sighs, thinking he is just tired, and chooses a seat next to the window.

With a loud, metallic screech, the train doors close automatically. A sharp whistle blows in the distance, and the heavy train begins to move slowly out of the station. It accelerates quickly, leaving the bright lights of the city behind and entering the pitch-black countryside. Mark stares at his own reflection in the dark window and tries to relax.

Strona 3

Chapter 2: The Red Notebook

Mark closes his eyes and tries to get some sleep during the forty-minute journey. However, just ten minutes later, the train suddenly brakes hard. The loud noise of grinding metal echoes through the carriage, and Mark is nearly thrown from his seat. The train comes to a complete stop. Mark checks his wristwatch. The hands point exactly to midnight.

Suddenly, the lights flicker twice and die. The entire carriage is plunged into total darkness. Mark realizes that the train’s engine has stopped completely; there is no hum of electricity, only the sound of heavy rain hitting the roof. He decides to use his smartphone as a flashlight. As he shines the light across the floor, he notices something unusual under the seat opposite him: a small red notebook.

Strona 4

Curiosity gets the better of him. Mark leans forward, picks up the dusty notebook, and opens it. The pages are filled with messy, hurried handwriting in red ink. It looks like a diary. He reads the first page: *“October 14th. I am trapped on this train. I know too much about the company's secret project, and now they are following me. If anyone finds this notebook, please take it to the police.”*

Mark feels a chill run down his spine. He turns to the next page. The date written at the top is today’s date: *“October 15th. They have cut the power to the train. There is no escape. Whatever you do, do not go to Cabin 7 in the rear carriage. He is waiting there.”* Mark’s heart begins to race. Is this a sophisticated prank, or is he in serious danger?

Strona 5

Chapter 3: Footsteps in the Dark

Before Mark can process the terrifying message, a loud *CLANG* echoes from the back of the train. It sounds like a heavy metal door being forced open. Mark freezes, holding his breath. He turns off his smartphone light so he won't be seen. In the absolute darkness, his hearing becomes incredibly sharp. He listens intently.

From two carriages away, he hears the distinct sound of footsteps. They are slow, heavy, and deliberate. *Thud... thud... thud...* Someone is walking through the train, moving from the rear towards his position. Mark realizes with horror that the person must have come from Cabin 7. The warnings in the red notebook are real.

Strona 6

Mark’s survival instincts kick in. He cannot stay in his seat; he is too exposed. He stands up quietly, making sure his shoes don't make a sound on the floor. He looks around for a place to hide. At the end of the carriage, there is a small luggage compartment with a plastic curtain. He slips inside and pulls the curtain closed, leaving just a tiny gap to look through.

A few moments later, the door to his carriage slides open with a soft hiss. A bright beam of light cuts through the darkness. The stranger has a powerful flashlight. Through the gap in the curtain, Mark can see the silhouette of a tall man wearing a long black coat and a hat. The man is holding a small, sharp tool in his left hand.

Strona 7

Chapter 4: The Discovery

The mysterious man walks slowly down the aisle. He shines his flashlight on every single seat, searching for something—or someone. Mark’s heart is pounding so loudly against his chest that he is terrified the stranger will hear it. He presses himself hard against the cold metal wall of the compartment, trying to become invisible.

The man stops right in front of the seat where Mark was sitting just moments ago. The flashlight beam drops to the floor. Mark realizes he made a terrible mistake: in his rush to hide, he dropped the red notebook right in the middle of the aisle. The tall man bends down, picks up the notebook, and places it inside his coat pocket.

Strona 8

The man utters a low, angry curse. He knows the passenger is nearby because the notebook is dry, meaning someone was reading it recently. The stranger turns slowly toward the luggage compartment. The beam of the flashlight moves across the floor, closer and closer to the plastic curtain behind which Mark is hiding. Mark prepares himself to fight for his life.

Suddenly, a loud mechanical alarm begins to ring from the front of the train. The emergency system has detected a fault. The sudden noise startles the stranger, who hesitates. He turns away from the curtain and looks toward the front carriage. Seizing this perfect distraction, Mark knows this is his only chance to escape.

Strona 9

Chapter 5: The Ultimate Escape

Mark pushes the curtain open and runs in the opposite direction, toward the rear of the train. The stranger shouts angrily and gives chase, his heavy boots echoing loudly behind Mark. Mark runs through the next carriage, his adrenaline pumping. He reaches the emergency exit door, but the electronic lock is dead without power.

He looks around desperately and notices the red emergency hammer mounted on the wall. He grabs it and smashes the glass panel of the door with all his strength. The glass shatters into a thousand pieces. The cold rain and wind rush into the carriage. Behind him, the stranger is only a few meters away, reaching out with his hand.

Strona 10

Without hesitating, Mark jumps out of the broken door into the dark, wet grass beside the tracks. He rolls down a small hill, his clothes getting covered in mud. He quickly hides behind a large bush and looks back at the train. The tall man stands at the broken door, shining his flashlight into the dark forest, but he cannot see Mark.

A few minutes later, the train's auxiliary power suddenly comes back on. The engines roar to life, and the train slowly begins to move again, carrying the mysterious stranger away into the night. Mark sits in the rain, breathing heavily but safely. He has survived the midnight train, but he knows his life will never be the same again.
