Chapter 1:
The Missing Bag

Max is a secret agent. He works for a top-secret government organization. He is tall, strong, and he has quick brown eyes that see everything. Today, Max is at the big international airport. The airport is very busy. There are thousands of people from different countries, loud announcements about flights, and heavy bags everywhere. Max wears a dark blue jacket, black sunglasses, and a special silver watch with a hidden communicator inside. He is not here to fly to another country; he is here for a very important and dangerous mission.

He stands near a busy coffee shop and looks around carefully. He spots a man sitting alone at a small table. This man has a large silver suitcase and a small red bag. Inside that small red bag, there is a dangerous computer chip. The chip contains secret data that can harm the whole city. Max watches the man closely. Suddenly, a strange person in a long grey coat walks very fast past the table. In one fast second, the grey coat grabs the red bag from the chair and runs quickly towards the exit doors. The silent airport turns into a dangerous zone. Max throws his coffee away. The chase begins now.

Strona 2

Max acts immediately. He has excellent reflexes. He touches the main button on his silver watch to activate his backup team. "The target is running fast to the main exit door. He has the red bag," Max speaks softly but clearly into his wrist. He jumps over a long row of blue airport chairs to save time. People shout in surprise, and a woman drops her fashion magazine on the floor. Max does not look back at them; his eyes are locked on the grey coat in front of him. He runs like a professional athlete through the crowd.

The thief runs out of the big glass airport doors and enters the warm morning sun. Max is fast, so he is only ten meters behind him now. The thief looks back, sees Max, and looks very scared. The thief runs directly into a busy city street. The street is full of yellow taxis, big delivery trucks, and red buses. Horns blow loudly as the thief dodges the fast traffic. Max takes a deep breath, ignores the danger, and runs into the busy street after him. He wants to save the city, so he cannot stop now.

Strona 3

A yellow taxi stops very fast with a loud, terrible screech of its tires. The driver opens his window and yells angrily, but Max is already on the other side of the dangerous road. The thief looks desperate. He turns left into a narrow, dark alley between two tall brick buildings. Max smiles because he knows this part of the city very well. This specific alley is a trap for people who do not know the area. It is a dead end with a very tall wooden fence at the very back. There is no way out.

Max arrives at the entrance of the dark alley. He stops running and walks slowly and quietly now. He listens to the sounds around him very carefully. He hears a heavy breathing sound coming from behind some big black rubbish bins. "Stop right there," Max says with a strong, calm voice. "Give me the red bag now, and nobody gets hurt. The game is over." The alley is completely quiet for one long second, and Max prepares his body for action. Then, he hears a sudden, violent movement behind the bins.

Strona 4

Chapter 2:
The Rooftop Chase

The thief does not surrender to Max. Instead, he throws a heavy metal bin lid directly at Max's head. Max ducks his head quickly, and the heavy lid hits the brick wall with a loud bang. The thief uses this short second to climb an old iron fire escape ladder on the side of the tall building. He moves very fast because he is full of adrenaline, climbing up towards the roof. Max runs to the ladder, grabs the cold iron bars, and starts to climb up behind him immediately. He is not afraid of heights.

The iron ladder shakes violently under Max's heavy boots. Suddenly, one old step breaks with a loud noise, but Max catches himself with his strong hands. He climbs higher and higher, looking only up. Finally, he reaches the flat roof of the tall building. The wind is very strong up here, blowing his hair across his face. The thief is already running across the roof. He jumps over big ventilation pipes and old television wires, heading fast towards the next building. Max runs after him with maximum speed.

Strona 5

The distance between the two tall buildings is about two meters. It is a very dangerous jump, high above the hard concrete of the city streets. The thief does not stop to think. He runs fast, jumps with all his power, and lands hard on the next roof. He rolls on the small stones to protect his body, stands up quickly, and continues running. The red bag is tight in his right hand. Max arrives at the edge of the roof and looks down for one short microsecond. He sees the tiny cars moving below like toys.

Max does not feel any fear; he only feels focus and adrenaline. He runs back three steps to get a good start. Then, he runs forward with max power and flies through the air like a bird. He lands perfectly on his feet on the next building's roof. He does not stop his movement for a second. He runs faster now, closing the distance between himself and the running thief. "Stop!" Max yells, but the thief keeps running across the gravel roof toward a small door.

Strona 6

The thief looks back again and cannot believe his eyes. Max is incredibly close now. The thief runs to a heavy metal roof door, pushes it open with his shoulder, and enters the building. He runs down a dark, steep flight of concrete stairs inside. Max follows him into the complete darkness immediately. The only sound in the building is the loud, heavy echo of their running boots on the hard steps. It feels like a dark labyrinth with no end, and they are moving down very fast.

They go down one floor, two floors, then three floors. Max can hear the thief's boots below him. Suddenly, the thief opens a heavy wooden door on the third floor and enters a large, old clothing factory. The factory is empty today because it is Sunday. There are long rows of white sewing machines, boxes of colorful fabric, and large cutting tables everywhere. It is a perfect place to hide from someone, and Max knows he must be very careful now. He slows down his steps.

Strona 7

Chapter 3:
The Empty Factory

The inside of the factory is dark because the big windows are very dirty. Dark shadows are everywhere. Max walks inside quietly, and the heavy wooden door closes behind him with a loud click. He takes a small blue laser torch from his jacket pocket. The thin blue light cuts through the dust in the air. Max moves slowly between the rows of old tables. He stops and listens, but he does not hear any footsteps now. The thief is hiding somewhere in this big room.

"I know you are here," Max says loudly. His strong voice echoes in the big, empty factory room. He looks under the long tables and behind the big wooden boxes of fabric. Suddenly, a heavy box of red cloth falls from a high shelf on his left side. Max jumps back quickly as the box hits the floor with a massive boom. It is a distraction! Max looks toward the windows and sees the thief running out from behind a large green curtain.

Strona 8

The big window at the back of the factory is broken now, and it leads to an old wooden balcony. The thief breaks the remaining glass with a heavy wooden chair and jumps outside onto the balcony. Max runs to the broken window, careful not to touch the sharp glass with his hands. He looks out. The thief is on the old balcony, but the wood is very old and weak. Max suddenly hears a loud, scary cracking sound from the structure. The balcony is breaking apart under the thief's weight!

The thief screams in terror as the wooden floor completely disappears under his feet. He falls, but his hands desperately catch the old iron railing of the balcony. He is now hanging in the air, three floors above the hard ground. The red bag is stuck on a broken piece of wood near his feet, dangling dangerousy. The thief cannot hold the iron railing for long because his hands are sweaty and slipping. He forgets about the escape. He needs help to survive.

Strona 9

Max acts like a true hero. He does not hesitate to save a life, even a thief's life. He climbs out of the broken window onto the remaining solid concrete part of the building. He lies down flat on his stomach and extends his strong right arm down towards the hanging man. "Take my hand now!" Max yells loudly over the sound of the strong wind. The thief looks up at Max with pure terror in his eyes. He leaves the railing and reaches up with his left hand to catch Max.

Max catches the thief's wrist with a very tight, professional grip. With a great physical effort, Max pulls the heavy man up onto the safe, solid concrete window ledge. The thief is safe now, but he is completely exhausted, shocked, and sits on the floor, breathing heavily. Max does not waste time. He quickly reaches down and grabs the red bag from the broken piece of wood. He opens the black zipper carefully and checks the contents inside.

Strona 10

Chapter 4:
Safe Data

Inside the red bag, the small black computer chip is completely safe and undamaged. Max closes the bag, takes a deep breath, and smiles happily. He looks at his silver watch and presses the main button again to call his office. "Mission success. I have the secret chip and the target is secure. Send the backup team to the old factory building on 5th Street immediately," Max says. A friendly voice answers from the watch speaker: "Good job, Max. The team is coming to your location right now."

Ten minutes later, two black police cars arrive outside the old building with flashing blue lights and loud sirens. Two strong officers walk up to the third floor of the factory and take the thief away in handcuffs. Max stands by the window and watches the police cars leave the area. He feels very tired from the long chase, but he is extremely happy. His beautiful city is safe again tonight. He takes the red bag with the chip, walks downstairs, and enters his car to go home and rest.
