Chapter 1: The Red Bicycle

Tom is a young boy. He is twelve years old. He lives in a small town with his mother, his father, and his big dog. The dog's name is Buddy. Buddy is brown and very friendly. Tom loves Buddy, and Buddy loves Tom.

Every day, Tom goes to school. He walks to school with his friends. But Tom has a big dream. He wants a bicycle. He wants a beautiful, red bicycle. "A bicycle is fast," Tom thinks. "I can go to the park on a bicycle."

Strona 2

On Saturdays, Tom goes to the big shop in the town center. The shop has many things. It has toys, books, clothes, and bicycles. Tom stands near the window. He looks at the red bicycle. It is perfect. It has a small bell and bright lights.

The bicycle is expensive. It costs one hundred pounds. Tom looks at his small money box at home. He has only ten pounds. "Ten pounds is not enough," Tom says softly. He is sad. Buddy sits next to Tom and looks at him with big eyes.

Strona 3

Tom talks to his father in the evening. "Dad, I want the red bicycle from the shop," Tom says. His father smiles but shakes his head. "I am sorry, Tom. One hundred pounds is a lot of money. We cannot buy the bicycle now. But you can work and save money."

"How can I work?" Tom asks. "I am only twelve years old." His father thinks for a minute. "Mr. Green is an old man. He lives in the big house near the park. He needs help in his garden. You can talk to him."

Strona 4

Chapter 2: Mr. Green's Garden

On Sunday morning, Tom goes to Mr. Green's house. Buddy walks with him. The house is very big, but the garden is old and messy. There are many yellow leaves on the green grass. Mr. Green sits on a chair on the porch. He wears a warm hat.

"Good morning, Mr. Green," Tom says. "My name is Tom. I live down the street. Do you need help in your garden?" Mr. Green looks at Tom and then looks at Buddy. Buddy sits down and is very quiet. Mr. Green smiles.

Strona 5

"Hello, Tom," Mr. Green says. "Yes, I am old, and my back hurts. Look at these leaves. They are everywhere. Can you clean the garden? I can pay you five pounds every Sunday." Tom is very happy. Five pounds is good money!

"Yes, sir! I can do it," Tom says. Mr. Green gives Tom a big plastic bag and a garden tool. Tom starts his work. He puts the old leaves into the big bag. Buddy watches Tom and barks happily. The sun is warm in the blue sky.

Strona 6

Tom works for three hours. He is tired, and his hands are dirty. But the garden looks clean now. The grass is beautiful and green. Mr. Green walks around the garden. He looks at Tom's work. "Very good, Tom. You are a good boy," the old man says.

Mr. Green opens his wallet and gives Tom a five-pound note. Tom says thank you. He runs home happily with Buddy. He puts the five pounds into his money box. Now he has fifteen pounds. "The red bicycle is closer," Tom thinks before he goes to sleep.

Strona 7

Chapter 3: The Secret Box

Four weeks pass. Every Sunday, Tom goes to Mr. Green's house. He cleans the garden, waters the beautiful flowers, and fixes the old wooden fence. Mr. Green likes Tom. Sometimes they drink hot tea and eat sweet biscuits together after work.

Now, Tom has thirty-five pounds in his money box. It is a good start, but he needs sixty-five pounds more. One afternoon, Tom finishes his work in the garden. Mr. Green says: "Tom, can you help me inside the house today? I need to move some old boxes in the garage."

Strona 8

Tom goes into the dark garage. There are many old things there. There are old newspapers, dirty tools, and big wooden boxes. Tom moves three boxes to the wall. Suddenly, behind a large box, Tom sees a very small, grey metal box. It has a key in the lock.

The metal box is dusty. Tom cleans the dust with his hand. He looks at the box. "Mr. Green, what is this?" Tom asks. Mr. Green walks into the garage. He looks at the grey box, and his face changes. He looks surprised and very emotional.

Strona 9

"Oh, my," Mr. Green says softly. "This is my old memory box. I lost it fifteen years ago! I looked for it everywhere. My old photographs and my gold watch are inside this box. They are very important to me. Thank you, Tom! You found my treasure."

Mr. Green takes the box and opens it with the small key. Inside, there are old black-and-white pictures of a young woman and a small boy. There is also a beautiful, shiny gold watch. Mr. Green smiles, and a small tear is in his eye. He is very grateful.

Strona 10

Chapter 4: The Surprise

The next Saturday is Tom's birthday. He is thirteen years old today. Tom wakes up early in the morning. He sees his mother and father in the kitchen. They have a small chocolate cake with candles. "Happy birthday, Tom!" they say.

Tom blows out the candles. He is happy, but he thinks about the red bicycle. He knows he does not have enough money yet. Suddenly, the doorbell rings. *Ding-dong!* Buddy runs to the front door and barks loudly. Tom opens the door.

Strona 11

Mr. Green stands on the porch. He is not alone. Next to him is a brand new, beautiful, shiny red bicycle! It has a big yellow ribbon on the handlebars. Tom opens his mouth in surprise. He cannot speak. He just looks at the bicycle.

"Happy birthday, Tom," Mr. Green says with a big smile. "You are a wonderful boy, and you found my secret memory box. This is a gift for you. You do not need to wait." Tom feels a great wave of joy. "Thank you, Mr. Green! Thank you so much!"

Strona 12

The End

Tom takes the red bicycle and goes to the park. Buddy runs next to him. The bicycle is very fast, and the bell makes a loud, happy sound. Tom's friends see him and wave their hands. Tom smiles under the bright sun. His dream is real now.

This is the story of Tom, his friendly dog Buddy, and the red bicycle. Hard work and a good heart always bring good things. Thank you for reading this story! Keep reading and learning every day!
