Chapter 1: The First Day

Adam wakes up at six o'clock in the morning. He is very excited today. It is his first day at his new job. He gets out of bed and goes to the bathroom. He washes his face and brushes his teeth. The water is cold and refreshing.

Adam goes to the kitchen. He makes a cup of strong black coffee and eats two pieces of toast with butter and jam. He looks out the window. The sky is gray, and it is raining a little bit, but Adam is happy. He puts on his new blue shirt and black pants.

Strona 2

At seven o'clock, Adam takes his umbrella and his bag. He walks to the bus stop. The bus stop is near his house, just three minutes away. Many people are waiting for the bus. Everyone has an umbrella, and nobody is talking. It is a quiet morning.

The big red bus arrives at seven fifteen. Adam gets on the bus and sits near the window. He looks at the city. There are many cars, shops, and restaurants. The journey takes twenty minutes. Adam gets off the bus in front of a big office building.

Strona 3

Chapter 2: The New Office

The office building is very modern and clean. It has twenty floors and big glass windows. Adam walks inside. A woman at the reception desk smiles at him. "Good morning. Can I help you?" she asks. "Good morning," Adam says. "My name is Adam. Today is my first day."

The woman looks at her computer. "Ah, yes! Welcome, Adam. You work on the fifth floor. Take the elevator over there." Adam says thank you and walks to the elevator. He presses the button for the fifth floor. His heart beats fast because he is a bit nervous.

Strona 4

The elevator opens on the fifth floor. It is a big, open office. There are many desks, computers, and plants. About thirty people are working there. Some people are typing on their keyboards, and others are talking on the phone. It is very busy.

A man walks to Adam. He is short, has brown hair, and wears a friendly smile. "Hello, Adam! I am Peter, the manager. Welcome to our team." Peter shakes Adam's hand warmly. "Come with me, I want to show you your desk and introduce you to your colleagues."

Strona 5

Chapter 3: Meeting the Team

Peter walks Adam to a nice desk near a large window. There is a new computer, a black notebook, and a blue pen on the desk. Next to Adam's desk sits a young woman with long blonde hair and bright blue eyes. She is typing an email.

"Adam, this is Sarah," Peter says. "Sarah is an expert here. She can help you today if you have any questions." Sarah stops typing and smiles nicely at Adam. "Nice to meet you, Adam," she says. "Welcome to the office. If you need anything, just ask me."

Strona 6

Adam sits down at his new desk. He turns on the computer and creates a password. For the first two hours, Sarah explains the work to him. She shows him the company website and how to use the special software. The work is interesting, but there are many new things to learn.

Adam writes everything down in his black notebook. He wants to be a good worker. At eleven o'clock, he feels a little tired. His brain is full of new information. "Do you want to take a short break?" Sarah asks. "Yes, please," Adam replies. "That sounds great."

Strona 7

Chapter 4: Lunch Break

They go to the office kitchen. The kitchen is small but very nice. It has a fridge, a microwave, and a big coffee machine. Two other colleagues are there. Their names are Mark and Lucia. They are drinking tea and eating biscuits.

"Hi, Adam!" Mark says. "Sarah told us about you. Welcome!" They all sit together at a small table. Adam drinks a glass of orange juice and eats an apple. They talk about their hobbies. Mark likes playing football, and Lucia loves cooking Italian food.

Strona 8

Adam tells them about his life. "I live alone in a small apartment near the park. In my free time, I like riding my bicycle and reading historical books," he says. Sarah says, "Oh, I love reading too! We have a small book club in the office. You can join us if you want."

Adam smiles. "Really? That is wonderful. I would love to join." He feels very comfortable now. The people in the office are very kind and friendly. He is not nervous anymore. After twenty minutes, they finish their break and go back to work.

Strona 9

Chapter 5: Going Home

The rest of the day goes very fast. Adam answers some emails and practices using the software. He makes a few small mistakes, but Sarah helps him and says, "Don't worry, it is your first day. You are doing a very good job." At five o'clock, the working day finishes.

People start to close their computers and pack their bags. "Goodbye, Adam. See you tomorrow!" Sarah says as she leaves. "Goodbye, Sarah. Thank you for your help today," Adam says. He turns off his computer and puts his notebook in his bag.

Strona 10

Adam walks out of the office building. The rain has stopped, and the sun is shining now. The air smells fresh. He walks to the bus stop and catches the red bus back home. He is tired, but he has a big smile on his face.

When he arrives at his apartment, he changes into comfortable clothes. He makes a simple dinner: pasta with tomato sauce. He eats his dinner and watches a movie on TV. "It was a wonderful day," Adam thinks before he goes to sleep. "I love my new life."
